## Alphabet

### Summary

- **ДҚ, ЕЎ, Еҫ**: Czech, Slovakia, Slovenia, Croatian, Baltics
- In Nordic countries:
  - **Г…**: All
  - **Гё, ГҰ**: Norway, Denmark, Faroe
  - **Г¶, ГӨ**: Sweden, Finland
  - **Г¶, ГҰ**: Iceland

## Romance languages

### Spanish

- **y** for and
- -ciГіn, -dad, -miento
- todas direcciones
- supermercado
- calle, avenida

### Catalan

- **i** for and
- -ciГі, -tat, -ment
- totes direccions
- supermercat
- carrer, avinguda

### Portuguese

- **e** for and
- -Г§ГЈo, -dade, -mente, -ismo

### Galician

- RГәa (without accent in portuguese)
- Town name with O, A articles: **A Pena, O Burgo**
- **concello** for municipality

## Basque

An isolated language

- Northern Spain to Southern France
- Common letters: **k, x, z, tx**
- **kalea** for street


## Celtic languages

### Welsh

- common letters: **ll, dd, w, y**
- accents: circumflex (ЛҶ), acute (Вҙ)

### Scottish Gaelic

- common letters: **bh, dh, fh, mh**
- accents: grave (`)

### Irish

- Use accents frequently: acute (Вҙ)
